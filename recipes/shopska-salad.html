Shopska salad

Rakia and shopska, a match made in heaven.

Shopska and rakia are meant to be, they are the perfect couple, the never ending love!

When making this salad, make sure that your tomatoes are juicy because that is what makes this salad so amazing. We don't add fancy salad dressings. It's oil and vinegar. But you don't need anything else because all the flavors blend so well together. It's like a flavor explosion!

Ingredients:

  • 3 to 4 ripe tomatoes , chopped
  • 1 long English cucumber , chopped
  • 1 medium yellow onion , chopped
  • green banana peppers, chopped
  • salt , to taste
  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2- cup crumbled white [feta] cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Recipe instructions:

  1. Place chopped tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, and peppers in a large serving bowl.
  2. Add salt, and oil to the tomato mixture.
  3. Toss and mix until well blended. Taste for seasoning and adjust accordingly.
  4. Top with crumbled feta cheese and sprinkle with fresh chopped parsley.
  5. Refrigerate for about 20 minutes, or until ready to use.
  6. Serve.
