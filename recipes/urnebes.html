Urnebes

Urnebes, meaning chaos, disorder, hilarious.

Nothing describes better the Balkan than this salad, appetizer, main dish, dessert. Don't get confused, urnebes is all of this things. It's really a chaos, but a chaos with taste.

Ingredients:

  • ajvar
  • white feta cheese
  • garlic
  • walnuts
  • sunflower oil or olive
  • chili pepper (optional)

Recipe instructions:

  1. Mash the feta cheese with a fork.
  2. Clean the garlic, chop it finely or press it using a garlic press.
  3. Mix both these ingredients.
  4. Add ajvar, walnuts, oil and the chili pepper
  5. Mix them.
  6. Enjoy.
